Delicious Dish

Description

This dish is a mouthwatering combination of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. It features a perfect balance of ingredients and is sure to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 pound round steak
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour for coating

Steps

  1. Step 1:Tenderize steak by pounding with a mallet. Cut into 3 inch long strips.
  2. Step 2:Combine egg, milk, salt and pepper in a shallow dish, whisk until well blended.
  3. Step 3:In a large skillet over medium heat, heat 1/3 cup oil (or just enough to cover the bottom of the pan).
  4. Step 4:Coat steak pieces in flour. Shake off excess. Then dip in the egg mixture and again in flour.
  5. Step 5:Coat steak pieces in flour. Shake off excess. Then dip in the egg mixture and again in flour.
